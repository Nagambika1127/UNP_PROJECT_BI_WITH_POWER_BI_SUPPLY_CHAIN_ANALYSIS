# UNP_PROJECT_BI_WITH_POWER_BI_SUPPLY_CHAIN_ANALYSIS
I did this project in collaboration with UNP, where we worked on analyzing a supply chain dataset using Microsoft Power BI.
The main objective of this project was to understand the overall business performance by analyzing different aspects such as revenue, profit, sales demand, supplier efficiency, manufacturing performance, transportation efficiency, and product-level insights.
To achieve this, we created multiple interactive dashboards. The revenue and profit dashboards helped us understand where the company is generating income and how efficiently it is earning. The sales dashboard provided insights into demand patterns across customer segments, locations, suppliers, and product categories.
We also analyzed supplier performance based on lead time, defect rate, and shipping cost to identify efficient and high-risk suppliers. The manufacturing dashboard focused on production cost, production time, and output capacity, while the transportation dashboards helped us evaluate the performance of different transportation modes and delivery routes in terms of cost, time, and profitability.
Additionally, the product analysis dashboard gave insights into inventory levels, production volume, demand, and quality across different product categories.
Based on the overall analysis, we identified key business issues such as high defect rates, longer lead times, and higher shipping costs, and suggested data-driven recommendations to improve operational efficiency, reduce costs, and enhance overall supply chain performance.
